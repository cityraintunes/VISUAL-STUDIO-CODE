My Journey Learning Code








My Journey learning code started out as a simple one. I decided that I was stuck in my career and no longer wanted to be at the whim of industry wind. Were I to stay in my current field I would be capped out at a certain salary. Given that I am going to be a dad, I want to maximize my income for my family. I want to make as much money as I can so that I can provide for my family.


It is essential to me to provide for my family. To be the best dad I can. I also know that I am smart and can learn things. I no longer have the passion for music like I used to. I want to transfer that passion into something like coding. Which is something I can do long term to increase both my brain power and earnings potential.


I want to learn how to code primarily so I can replace the habit of playing video games with the habit of coding. I will get the same sense of satisfaction at completing a code, or learning a new trick, as I would with beating a boss. I won't entirely stop playing games, as I still enjoy playing them, but coding should provide me with real world skills that could help me advance in my career. This makes me happy and feel good.
